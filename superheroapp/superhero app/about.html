About the Avengers

The Avengers are a superhero team in the Marvel Cinematic Universe formed to protect Earth from global threats. Initially assembled in 2012 by S.H.I.E.L.D to defeat Loki and the Chitauri army, they have since expanded their roster and faced numerous challenges.

  • Formation: The Avengers were formed in response to the threat posed by Loki and the Chitauri invasion.

  • Purpose: Their primary goal is to protect global stability from both internal and external threats.

  • Membership: The initial team consisted of Captain America, Iron Man, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye. The roster has since evolved with new members being added.

  • Screen Appearances: The Avengers have appeared in multiple movies, including "The Avengers," "Avengers: Age of Ultron," "Avengers: Infinity War," and "Avengers: Endgame," among others.

  • Ongoing Development: The Avengers' story continues in the MCU, with new installments planned, such as "Avengers: Doomsday" and "Avengers: Secret Wars," according to Marvel Studios.
